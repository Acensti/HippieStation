29 July 2020

carlopault updated:

  • chapdrobe displays an emag message if you examine it after it is emagged.
  • yakama now gives you the special scream instead of the haramaki armor
  • 5 new recipes for the curator's various obelisks (most of them were already created, I just forgot to add them to the obelisk code)
  • The curator's obelisks can now be used once per obelisk to create 1 use books that teach everything of the respective tier. This means other people can learn how to make the obelisks now (without buying another book from the Spacestar Ordering Book Catalog).
  • CODEWORD Arabian now only has a 50% chance of dropping an improvised glock.
  • Nerfed the health of the curator's obelisks.
  • Fixed the d100 recipe spawning a d00 die.
  • The obelisks now have sound effects.
  • Added icons for the books. They were sprited by Crunion and Punkinguy.
  • Fixed a typo with CODEWORD Arabian's description.

27 July 2020

carlopault updated:

  • The chaplain's wardrobe vendor can now be emagged to acquire a samurai armaments beacon.
  • I have coded an entire samurai set of clothing. This includes a suit, armor, samurai sword, tanto dagger, belt, shoes, etc.
  • The samurai armor allows you to ignore slowdown caused by damage.
  • The costume vendor in the backstage room can now be used to buy a yakama (samurai clothing).
  • The samurai armor gives you a special scream.
  • Sprites for everything were done by Crunion (Shazbot)
  • fixed a typo in the wooden stake's description
